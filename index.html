Chicken

ndian chicken curry typically starts with whole spices, heated in oil. A sauce is then made with onions, ginger, garlic, and tomatoes, and powdered spices. Bone-in pieces of chicken are then added to the sauce, and simmered until cooked through. In South India, coconut and curry leaves are also popular ingredients.

Beef

Add oil, chopped onions, garlic, spices, salt and pepper. ... Then brown the meat on all sides, pour in the blended spice paste, stock and add lemon zest. Cover the lid and cook for 30 minutes on high pressure. Serve this incredibly tender and flavorful beef masala curry with steamed rice and chopped cilantro.
